Q1. What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO ?
Ans. The full form of SEO is Search Engine Optimization. It helps in ranking the website on search engines by
    giving the relevant data to the search engine crawlers that helps in ranking and getting more traffic. It helps
    in overall performance of website in online web world. 

Q3. What are benefits of using meta tags ?
Ans. The meta tags are very much useful. These are not directly visible to users but these seen clearly by web crawlers 
    as meta tags provide to the point data to the crawlers and helps crawlers what the data is about and helps in ranking.
    These are also best for optimizing site for being shared on different social media platforms.

Q5. Define Favicon and give an illustrative example ?
Ans. A favicon is tiny icon present in the tab of the browser. These are also shown in the bookmarks.
    These are related to logo of website. These enhance the overall user experience.

    Example - 

        <!DOCTYPE html>
        <html>
            <head>
                <meta charset="UTF-8">
                <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
                <title>SEO Favicon</title>
                
                <link rel="icon" type="image/x-icon" href="favicon.ico">
            </head>
            <body>
            </body>
        </html>
